Computer Lab >> Facilities

Information Technology has been given an equal importance in the school-curriculum. In this regard, the school provides a well-furnished AC Centre computer Centre. All systems are latest and upgraded with new technology, connected on LAN with a centralized server. Systems are also capable of multimedia uses. Users can share the resources and peripheral from any of the systems. There is an access to internet round the clock as school has its own Broadband.


There is a Chinese proverb; "Tell me, I may forget. Show me, I may remember. But involve me and I will understand." We use Audio-visual room to show educational CDs on different subjects. Students’ interactive programmes and teachers’ workshop-cum-reorientation programmes are held at regular intervals.
