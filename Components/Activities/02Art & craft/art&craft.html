Art & craft >> Activities

Unnoticed, art permeates every aspect of human existence. The value of art and craft for pupils goes well beyond the classroom; via encouraging creativity, it adds to a well-rounded education. Along with fostering pupils’ self-worth and uniqueness, they impart life skills like tenacity, patience, and time management to their students. They not only improve people as individuals but also provide employment prospects in a world that is changing quickly. In summary, art and craft have a positive impact on pupils’ personalities, helping them to develop into confident, creative adults.
