Sports >> Activities

The sports complex of the school, with a number of courts for various indoor & outdoor games, bubble with mirth and enthusiasm of students in their strive for excellence in sports under the supervision of expert coaches and trainers. Facilities of indoor & outdoor games are available to the students without hindrances. Inter house & inter-school tournaments are organized from time to time & school teams are sent to participate at various levels of tournament.


Healthy mind resides in healthy body. Positive health, growth & development of a child rely upon the exposure he gets in the playground. It is mandatory for every student to take part in at least one game.
