 Teaching Aids

  • Interactive whiteboards are used in SGS School as replacements for traditional whiteboards of flipcharts. They provide ways to show students any thing which can be presented on a computers desktop (educational software, web sites, and other). In addition, interactive whiteboards allow teachers to record their instruction and post the material for review by students at a later time. This can be a very effective instructional strategy for students who benefit from repetition, who need to see the material presented again, for students who are absent from school, for struggling learners and for review of examinations.
