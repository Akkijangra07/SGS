Director Message


Dr. Col. Jagat Singh Tanwar

Director


A warm welcome to all students, parents, faculty, and visitors to SGS School.

As the Director of SGS School, it is with great pleasure and pride that I extend my greetings to each of you. At our school, we believe in fostering a community where every individual is empowered to thrive, grow, and excel in all aspects of their academic journey and personal development.

Our commitment to academic excellence, holistic education, and character building is unwavering. We strive to provide a nurturing environment where students are encouraged to explore their passions, think critically, and embrace challenges as opportunities for growth.

As we embark on another academic year, I encourage each student to approach their studies with curiosity, determination, and a spirit of collaboration. Remember, education is not just about acquiring knowledge.It's about developing the skills, values, and mindset to become lifelong learners and responsible global citizens.

To our dedicated faculty and staff, I extend my deepest gratitude for your tireless efforts and dedication to nurturing the next generation of leaders and innovators. Your passion, expertise, and unwavering commitment to excellence are the foundation of our school's success.

To the parents and guardians, thank you for entrusting us with the education and well-being of your children. We recognize the importance of your partnership and involvement in your child's educational journey, and we are committed to working together to ensure their success.

Together, let us embrace the opportunities and challenges that lie ahead with optimism, resilience, and a shared commitment to excellence. I am confident that, with our collective efforts, SGS School will continue to inspire, empower, and transform lives for years to come.

Warm regards,

Dr. Col. Jagat Singh Tanwar
